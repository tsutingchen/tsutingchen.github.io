About

I'm a marketing specialist with over seven years of experience. I work across social media, content, and paid ads. Right now I'm the sole marketing lead at MOSA Innovations, a smart cycling startup, where I run the full digital function for four brands.

I care about two things in my work. First, craft: every piece of copy should earn its place. Second, systems: every workflow can be smarter. I built an AI content tool that cut content production time by 80% and runs for less than £1 a month.

I speak English and Mandarin Chinese. I have a degree in Social Welfare from National Chung Cheng University. I take photographs on film when the world feels too fast.
